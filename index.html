RushPro + RushLive
Both Trainer and Trainee log into GymRush app. Trainers receive the RushPro experience. Trainees, RushLive experience. Both Trainer and Trainee use GymRush to connect, share, and maintain their fitness programs. Seamless connectivity between Trainer and Trainee – 24/7 reporting, user stat analytics, messaging, book appointments and more. All in the palm of your hand.
